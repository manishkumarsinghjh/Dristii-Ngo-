About Dristi

"Love the people, lift the people, people are the way. Not your people, not my people, it's all one people…"


Welcome to DRISTI, a dedicated non-profitable organization, committed to making a positive impact on our community and beyond by addressing pressing social, environmental, and humanitarian issues through innovative and collaborative initiatives.

Founded on the belief that every individual has the power to make a difference, we empower volunteers, partner with local communities, and advocate for meaningful policy changes to create a more just, equitable, and sustainable world. Our focus is directed towards the upliftment of rural India through social and technological integration, aiming to drive transformative change in the rural sector


With unwavering dedication and a shared vision for a brighter future, we invite you to join us on this journey of transformation and hope. Together, we can build a society that values social equity, ensuring a better tomorrow for all

Rain Guard Health Initiatives

Guarding Wellness Through Every Downpour

Extra Info:Date: 31th August, 2024 Venue: Gossner High School, Ranchi. Pillar: Disha (दिशा) Tagline: Guarding Wellness Through Every Downpour! Event Highlights: Raised awareness about mosquito-borne diseases like dengue and malaria. Interactive sessions, with the help of creative handmade posters, taught students how to stay safe using repellents, masks, and basic hygiene practices. A nukkad natak creatively portrayed the risks of neglect and the power of simple habits, enhanced by the display of creative handmade posters. Each of the 200+ students received a Health Protection Kit containing Odomos, a pair of gloves, a mask, hand sanitizer — and to sweeten the message, a chocolate and a smile.

The Pillars Of Dristi

DISHA

Disha is committed to providing direction to the people and enhancing their quality of life by instilling the values of cleanliness, personal hygiene, and duty towards the nation. By embracing these principles, individuals improve their personal as well as community well-being, thereby ensuring a healthier, more responsible, and united society.

DIKSHA

Diksha is dedicated to providing direction for educational growth and development, serving as a catalyst for expanding imaginations and fostering dreams. Through comprehensive educational initiatives and support, Diksha aims to cultivate a culture of continuous learning and intellectual advancement within the community.

DAKSHA

Daksha, reflects our commitment to creating an inclusive environment and empowering individuals. We organize computer classes, engaging art and crafts sessions, insightful career counseling, and a variety of recreational activities which not only enhance the knowledge of our participants but also boost their morale and foster intrinsic connection within our community.

DAMINI

Empowering women is not a single-day feat but a continuous thread of small efforts woven each day. The pillar Damini, underlines our aim to foster the self sustainable development of girls and women, nurturing their potential to flourish and thrive.
